⚛️ Quantum Superposition

A particle exists in multiple states until observed

50%

In quantum mechanics, a particle can exist in a superposition of multiple states simultaneously. The particle is described by a wavefunction |ψ⟩ = α|↑⟩ + β|↓⟩, where α and β are probability amplitudes. When we measure the particle, the wavefunction "collapses" to a single definite state.
